Sacred Journey: Durga Idol's Epic Voyage from India to Barcelona

The magnificent Durga idol has embarked on an extraordinary journey from the sacred lands of India, making its way through Ukraine, and finally reaching its destination in Barcelona, Spain. This spiritual pilgrimage represents the global reach of Bengali culture and the dedication of the Bengali community in Barcelona to preserve and celebrate their rich traditions.
