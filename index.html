About Durga Puja

Discover the significance and traditions of this sacred festival

The Divine Celebration

Durga Puja is one of the most significant festivals in Bengali culture, celebrating the victory of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura. This five-day festival symbolizes the triumph of good over evil and brings communities together in celebration.

Our Mission

The Bengali Cultural Association of Barcelona is dedicated to preserving and promoting Bengali culture, traditions, and values in Spain. Through events like Durga Puja, we create a home away from home for the Bengali community.
