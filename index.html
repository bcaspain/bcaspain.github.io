Who We Are

Bringing Bengal closer to Barcelona, one celebration at a time.

The Bengali Cultural Association of India in Spain is a registered non-profit organisation founded in 2021. We are a community of Bengalis and well-wishers who have made Spain our home, united by a shared love for our heritage, language, and traditions.

Our mission is to preserve and promote Bengali culture across Spain — through festivals, music, dance, literature, food, and service to the community. We welcome everyone who wishes to experience the warmth and colour of Bengal.
